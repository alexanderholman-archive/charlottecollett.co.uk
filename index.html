More information

Charlotte Collett is 25 and from Teignmouth, Devon.

She has a 2:1 BSc Environmental Science (Honours) from Plymouth University which was obtained in 2014 and a DDD Nation Diploma in Animal Management from Bridgwater College in 2011.

She has had experience and worked with some of the South West most influencial enviroment agencies, including the Whitley Wildlife Conservation Trust, the Shark Trust, Devon Wildlife Trust and SAHFOS.

Charlotte is an active campaigner for wildlife conservation and is regularly works to inprove the ethical standards of companies such as Morisons via campaigns on social media platform Twitter.
